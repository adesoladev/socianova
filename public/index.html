Frequently Asked Questions

We've kept things simple, but if you're wondering, here’s more.

What does Socianova do?

Socianova is a digital-first influencer marketing company that helps brands connect with authentic creators, manage campaigns, and grow through data-driven strategies.

Who are your typical clients?

We work with startups, SMEs, and established brands across industries, from fashion and beauty to tech and lifestyle — helping them scale through influencer partnerships and digital campaigns.

How do you select influencers for a campaign?

We analyze audience data, engagement rates, content quality, and brand fit to match your campaign with the right creators for authentic results.

What makes Socianova different from other influencer marketing agencies?

Our mix of data analytics, creative storytelling, and authentic creator relationships allows us to deliver measurable results — not just reach.

Can you handle campaigns for international brands?

Yes. We operate globally and can manage campaigns across multiple regions, languages, and social platforms.

How do you measure campaign success?

We track key metrics like engagement, conversions, audience growth, and ROI — providing detailed performance reports after every campaign.

Do you offer custom packages or pricing?

Yes. Every campaign is tailored to fit your brand goals, budget, and target audience. Get in touch to discuss a package that works for you.

How can I get started with Socianova?

Simply reach out through our contact form or email us at hello@socianova.com — our team will respond within 24 hours to discuss your campaign goals.
